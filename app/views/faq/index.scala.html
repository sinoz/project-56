This FAQ contains information related to ReStart and its store. If you cannot find an answer to your question, make sure to contact us.

General questions

Do I need to be registered to order a game-account on Restart?

No, you do not need to be registered to buy a game-account. If you want to sell or trade accounts however, you do need to be registered.

How do I buy a game-account?

If you wish to buy a game account you have to select the game you wish to buy. In the product page of the gamecategory you will see different types of accounts, select the account you want to buy. Click on buy and fill in your payment details. After Restart has taken care of the buying process you will receive an e-mail with the details of the account.

How do I become a seller?

1. On the main page click "Login"
2. Now click on the "Register" button
3. Fill in the form and click send!

You are now registered on the Restart website and able to sell accounts.
